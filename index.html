palak paneer

INGREDIENTS

palak
paneer
onion
garlic
ginger

method to cook>

Blach the palak for a minute and wash it under a cold water.Take some oil in a pan and add chopped garlic,ginger,onion.let it cook for few minutes.add tomato puree into it and add all masalas.add garam masalas and kasuri methi at the end and add coriander to it.
